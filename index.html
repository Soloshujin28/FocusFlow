About Focus Flow

Our Mission

At Focus Flow, we believe that productivity shouldn't come at the expense of well-being. Our mission is to help people work smarter, not harder, by providing tools that promote focus, balance, and sustainable productivity.

How It Works

Focus Flow uses the proven Pomodoro Technique, a time management method that breaks work into intervals, traditionally 25 minutes in length, separated by short breaks. This technique has been shown to improve focus, reduce burnout, and enhance productivity.

FAQ

What is the Pomodoro Technique?
The Pomodoro Technique is a time management method that uses a timer to break work into intervals, traditionally 25 minutes in length, separated by short breaks.
How do I track my progress?
Focus Flow automatically tracks your completed sessions and tasks, displaying your progress in easy-to-understand charts and statistics.
